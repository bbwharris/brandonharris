Talebait

This was an inherited project. The client requested an activities feed, a five star rating, an index for the open water section of the site and several small changes. It was also requested to setup a staging site for future changes

The site is hosted on Slicehost and during the updates, it became clear that the current slice could not support the traffic or the necessary resources.

I updated the backend to use Ruby Enterprise Edition with Nginx Passenger. The budget was tight so we had to squeeze every ounce out of the memory in the slice. Nginx + REE delivered and greatly improved the speed of the site.
